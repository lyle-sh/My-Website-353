About Lyle

The Racing Mindset

Racing chose me at age nine. From my first ancient kart to the polished machine beneath me today, every lap has been a lesson. Those formative years—soaking Saturday sessions in the rain, regional championships, the grinding competition circuit—weren't just about going fast. They were about learning how to think under pressure.

What separates champions from the rest? It's not talent. It's resilience.

Where Most Crack, I Accelerate

I've learned more from every crash, every DNF, every controversial decision than from any podium. Why? Because losing forces you to evolve. It teaches you to manage risk, read conditions, stay sharp when everything falls apart. In professional racing—and in life—that's the edge that matters.

The ability to bounce back from setback, to stay mentally sharp when circumstances shift, to extract performance from chaos: that's what defines my career. That's what I bring to every corner, every race, every partnership.

Built on Competition. Driven by Excellence.

I don't just drive. I compete. I improve. I deliver results.
